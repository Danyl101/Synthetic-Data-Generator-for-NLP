decline followed a large block deal in early trade, in which nearly 9.8 million shares of the company, worth approximately $165 million, were offloaded.According to the term sheet, the transaction represented 1% of Lodha's issued share capital.The floor price for the block deal was set at Rs 1,384.60 per share, reflecting a 4% discount to the previous day's closing price.The seller is believed to be an undisclosed institutional investor conducting a clean-up trade.Books for the deal closed at 6:00 am on Wednesday, and standard buy-side commissions of 25 basis points applied to the transaction.Goldman Sachs acted as the sole placing agent for the block sale.Lodha share price performance Over the past one year, the shares of Lodha have declined by 1.21%, while on a year-to-date
